Our Project

DOTbot

We came up with a chat bot for flight travel assistance. Using information from the Department of Transportation (DOT), this website application is helpful for any questions you may have about travelling via airplane.


DOTbot is trained from a variety of questions and answers from the DOT Aviation Consumer Protection website.
